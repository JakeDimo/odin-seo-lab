# What makes pages rank — AU test

We compared **103 pages Google ranks in the top 10** ('winners') against **136 pages stuck at positions 21–45** ('losers'), across 3 niches, to see what the winners actually do differently.

## Bottom line

**What separates winners (worth doing):**
- ✅ Outbound links to other sites
- ✅ Estimated monthly visits (page)

**What does NOT matter** — winners and losers do these equally, so don't over-optimise them. See the ➖ rows below.

**Still unknown:** backlinks / off-page authority — likely the real driver, not measured yet (deferred — would need DataForSEO's paid backlinks subscription).

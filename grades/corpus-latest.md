Corpus: **24050** graded pages — **4105** winners (rank 1-3), **9985** challengers (4-10), **9960** non-placers (21-45), across 10 niches.

> ⚠️ **Correlations, not proven rules.** Within-rank comparisons + page-type segmentation control for the obvious confounders, but nothing here is causal until a controlled live test confirms it. Traffic & ranked-keyword rows are `[ctrl]` (downstream of rank — context, not levers). Speed is read as CWV pass-rate.

- ✅ nav/footer links
- ✅ trust mentions
- ✅ outbound links
- ✅ referring domains
- ✅ Authority Score
- ✅ total backlinks

Everything else showed no link (see ➖ rows). Authority controls (`[ctrl]`) below are context, not levers.

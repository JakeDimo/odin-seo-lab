# What correlates with ranking — AU test (LEADS, not rules)

Compared **294 pages ranking in the top 10** ('winners') against **369 pages at positions 21–45** ('losers'), across 3 niches.

> ⚠️ **These are correlations, not proven rules.** A factor that shows no link here can still matter once we control for things not yet held constant (page speed, authority/backlinks, page type), and a factor that correlates is not proven to *cause* anything. Something becomes a rule only after it (1) holds across far more pages, (2) survives controlling for confounders, and (3) is confirmed by a controlled change on a live page. Treat every row below as a lead to test.

## Bottom line

**More common in winning pages here (leads to test, not proven):**
- ✅ Outbound links to other sites
- ✅ Layout shift / CLS (lower = better)
- ✅ Keywords this page ranks for
- ✅ Estimated monthly visits (page)

**Showed no link in this sample** — this is NOT proof they don't matter; confounders (speed, authority) aren't controlled yet, and the sample is still small. See the ➖ rows.

**Not measured yet:** backlinks / off-page authority (deferred).
